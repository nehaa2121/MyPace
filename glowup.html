6-Month "Beauty with Brain" Transformation Plan

SKINCARE:

  1. Hydration - Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily.
  2. Healthy Diet - Eat more fruits (like papaya, pomegranate, and oranges), vegetables, nuts(almonds,walnuts),and protein(dal, eggs, soybeans).
  3. Daily Skincare -
    • Morning (after waking up)
      1. Wash face with water/ mild face wash.
      2. Apply aloe vera gel (moisturizer).
      3. If going outside : Sunscreen
    • Daytime
      1. Drink plenty of water (keeps skin hydrated).
      2. Don't touch your face too much (reduces pimples).
      3. Dab excess oil with tissue if skin feels oily.
    • Evening Skincare(after coming from college/ outside)
      1. Clease Again
        • Wash face to remove dirt, pollution, and sweat.
      2. Moisturize
        • Reapply aloe vera gel or light cream.
    • Night Skincare
      1. Clease
        • Wash face before sleeping (never sleep with dirt/sweat on face).
      2. Moisturize/Repair
        • Apply aloe vera gel or a night cream.
        • Optional: Dab a little coconut oil/ Vaseline on lips.
  4. Weekly Skincare -
    • 2 times a week: Face mask
    • 1 time a week: Gentle scrub
    1. DIY:
    • besan+haldi+milk/honey
