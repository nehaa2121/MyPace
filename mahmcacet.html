1. Algebra

  • Basic Algebra
    • Simplification of expressions

    • Quadratic equations (ax² + bx + c = 0)

2. Calculus

  • Differential Calculus
    • Limits and continuity
    • Differentiation of:
      • Polynomials, exponential, logarithmic, trigonometric functions
    • Applications:
      • Maxima & minima
      • Tangent and normal to curves
      • Increasing/decreasing functions
  • Integral Calculus
    • Indefinite and definite integrals
    • Standard integration formulas
    • Applications:
      • Area under curves
      • Solving simple differential equations

3. Coordinate Geometry

  • Two-Dimensional Geometry
    • Distance formula, midpoint formula
    • Slope of line, equation of a line (point-slope, slope-intercept, two-point forms)
    • Equation of circle, parabola, ellipse, hyperbola
  • Straight Lines
    • Parallel & perpendicular lines
    • Intersection points
    • Angle between two lines

4. Trigonometric

  • Trigonometric ratios: sin, cos, tan, cosec, sec, cot
  • Trigonometric identities:
    • sin²θ + cos²θ = 1
    • tan²θ + 1 = sec²θ
    • 1 + cot²θ = csc²θ
  • Trigonometric equations
  • Heights and distances problems
  • Sine rule, Cosine rule

5. Probability & Statistics

  • Statistics
    • Mean, Median, Mode
    • Variance and standard deviation
  • Probability
    • Basic probability rules
    • Conditional probability
    • Simple problems
  • Combinatorics
    • Permutations and combinations

6. Set Theory & Relations

  • Sets and subsets
  • Union, intersection, complement
  • Venn diagrams
  • Relations and functions (domain, range)

7. Number Systems & Logarithms

  • Real numbers, integers, rational and irrational numbers
  • Logarithms: properties, conversion between exponential and logarithmic forms
  • Problems using laws of logarithms

8. Sequences & Series

  • Arithmetic progression (AP)
  • Geometric progression (GP)
  • Harmonic progression (HP)
  • Sum of squares, sum of cubes
  • Special series: 1 + 2 + ... + n

9. Miscellaneous Topics

  • Binomial theorem (for positive integer powers)
  • Complex numbers:
    • a + ib form
    • Addition, subtraction, multiplication, division
  • Linear inequalities (1 variable and 2 variables)
  • Simple word problems using algebra or calculus

✅ Preparation Tips

  1. Focus on practice: Solve MCQs from previous years first.
  2. Time management: Give 1–2 minutes per question in practice.
  3. Formula sheet: Make a small sheet for all formulas and identities.
  4. High-weight topics: Algebra, Calculus, Probability, and Coordinate Geometry appear most frequently.
  5. Revision: Revise formulas and tricky problems weekly.
