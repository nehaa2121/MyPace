Neha's Daily Checklist

Date:

Morning (MCA CET)

  • Solve 20 reasoning/math questions OR revise 1 topic
  • Vocabulary/English practice (10–15 mins)

Afternoon (College / Assignments)

  • Attend college / complete assignments
  • Quick refresh / snack

Evening (Exam Prep & Projects)

  • Revise 1 chapter / 1 subject
  • Solve past questions / practice problems
  • Project work: Write/test/debug 1 section

Night (Skill-building & Reflection)

  • Skill-building (Coding, AI, Portfolio)
  • Quick reflection: what went well & improvements

Optional

  • Exercise / walk / meditation (15–20 mins)
  • Reading / relaxation (15–20 mins)
